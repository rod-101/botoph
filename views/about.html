About BotoPH.edu

Empowering the Filipino vote through facts, not noise.

BotoPH.edu is a web-based platform aimed at empowering Filipino voters—especially the youth—with verified, non-partisan, and accessible information about upcoming elections, candidates, voting procedures, and voter rights.

Rationale

  • Many Filipinos rely on social media for news, on which fake information pervades.
  • Youth voters make up over 50% of the population but remain uninformed or disengaged.

Project Goals

  • Combat election misinformation
  • Encourage informed voting
  • Increase youth participation
  • Simplify access to official information

Core Features

  • Candidate Profiles:
    • Personal background, platform, political history
    • Linked to verified news (COMELEC, Rappler, etc.)
    • Searchable by name
  • Voter Registration Guide:
    • Step-by-step for first-time, transfer, correction
    • Requirements and deadlines
    • Link to COMELEC
  • Voter Rights:
    • Right to Vote (Suffrage)
    • Right to Be Informed
    • Right to Equal, Accessible Voting
    • Right to a Secret Ballot
    • Right to File Complaints
    • Right to Participate in Reform

Advanced Features

  • Fact Checking:
    • Paste a link or text to flag questionable content
    • Connects with VERA Files, Tsek.ph

Admin Panel

  • Admins can post and manage candidate profiles in real time.
